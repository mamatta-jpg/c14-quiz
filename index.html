🪤 Traps & High-Yield Tips

  • Time budget: ~72 sec/question (100 Q / 120 min). Flag-and-return on hard ones; leave 15–20 min to review flagged questions at the end.
  • DCPD deductible applies only when the insured is at fault. 0% fault → $0 deductible (insurer pays full repair, then subrogates).
  • Ontario mandatory minimum TPL = $200,000. ($100K is the old pre-1996 distractor.)
  • SABS medical/rehab is the baseline benefit — paid even if neither IRB nor non-earner benefit applies.
  • Uninsured Auto priority: 95% to bodily injury / 5% to property damage.
  • Misrepresentation does NOT require intent — incorrect or missing material info "with or without intent to mislead" qualifies.
  • Statutory Conditions: proof of loss = 90 days. Newly acquired auto auto-coverage = 14 days to notify.
  • Study 12: know government-vs-private and no-fault-vs-tort by province (e.g. SK = SGI Crown; QC public for bodily injury only).
